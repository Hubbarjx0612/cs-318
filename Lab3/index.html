Harry Potter consists of 7 books and 8 movies.
Harry Potter's life changes forever on his eleventh birthday when he discovers he is a wizard, an orphan famous in the magical world for surviving an attack by the dark wizard Lord Voldemort as a baby, an event that left him with a lightning-shaped scar and his parents dead.
Raised in a cupboard under the stairs by his neglectful aunt and uncle, the Dursleys, Harry receives an acceptance letter to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, embarking on a new life filled with magic, friendship with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and a looming threat as Voldemort seeks to regain power.

The Harry Potter film series, produced by Warner Bros. and based on J.K. Rowling's beloved novels, consists of eight fantasy films released between 2001 and 2011.
